ABOUT

Bob's Garage Door was started in 1968 by Bob and Jaenette Dickeson and has operated from its current location since 1977. The small family operation has consistently grown by providing high quality products and services at a fair price and standing behind the work we do. Their son, Tod, took over the business in May of 1995 after finishing school at the University of Idaho. Tod and his wife Jennifer have three children; Tanner who is currently serving in the Air Force and recently married, a U of I freshman daughter, Jordan, and a junior high daughter Julia.

The family is very active with local organizations such as the Boys and Girls Club, Jackson's Pay it Forward Foundation, and Community of Call. Jennifer serves on the Board of Directors for Potlatch Number 1 Federal Credit Union. The family is proud to be involved with this community and enjoy being a part of a great organizations that give back.
